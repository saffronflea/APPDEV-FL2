Welcome in Rice Farms Seminar

Rice farm workshops are an important part of new generational agricultural education. And was selected to contribute to the development of rural culture. We use traditional solutions, disinfectants, pesticides, water, and other post-storage conditions. Farm workers will increase their income by attending workshops. It is a joint venture with agricultural experts, fellow farmers, and scholars involved in intellectual agricultural trading and receives funding from the government itself. The group aims to support agriculture and food, increase income, and strengthen local communities. this seminar should help our farmers to reach their dreams.
